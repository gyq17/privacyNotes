Privacy Policy for PawSnap

Effective date:

PawSnap respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the PawSnap mobile application ("the App") handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

PawSnap does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. The App does not require user accounts and does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Camera Access

The App uses the device camera solely to allow users to take photos with their pets. All photos are processed and stored locally on the user's device. PawSnap does not upload photos or videos to any server.

Photo Sharing

PawSnap provides an optional feature that allows users to share photos using other applications (such as email or messaging apps). When you choose to share a photo, the photo is shared directly through the app you select. PawSnap does not access, store, or transmit shared content.

Audio and Sound

PawSnap may play sounds within the App to attract pet attention. These sounds are generated by the App and do not involve recording, accessing, or transmitting audio from the device microphone.

Children’s Privacy

PawSnap is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

PawSnap does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
